CR7-bot is the ultimate interactive companion for fans of the legendary footballer Cristiano Ronaldo. Designed to be a comprehensive encyclopedia at your fingertips, this bot provides instant and detailed answers to a wide range of questions about CR7’s illustrious career, record-breaking stats, and personal milestones. From his early days at Sporting CP to his historic runs at Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus, and Al-Nassr, CR7-bot offers deep insights into the life and achievements of one of the greatest athletes of all time. Whether you are looking for specific match data, trophy tallies, or fun trivia, this bot serves as your go-to source for everything related to the legacy of Cristiano Ronaldo.

You need Python and Ollama to run this on local. Create a data folder in the root. Data would be fetched from latest wikipedia but you can add .pdf or .txt files in the data folder to add more sources
